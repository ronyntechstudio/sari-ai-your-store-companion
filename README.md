Privacy Policy for Sari Store
Effective Date: February 21, 2026 Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction
Sari Store ("the App") is developed and operated by Ronyn Tech Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play.

By using Sari Store, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
a. Account Information
•
Full name and email address used during sign-up or sign-in
•
Store name and business details you provide
b. Business Data
•
Inventory items, product names, prices, and stock levels
•
Sales transactions and payment records
•
Customer credit records (utang/credit tracking)
•
Reports and analytics data generated within the app
c. Media & Camera
•
Product images captured or uploaded by you for inventory management
•
The app requests camera permission solely for scanning and photographing products
d. Team & Collaboration Data
•
Names and email addresses of team members you invite to your store
•
Role assignments and access permissions
e. Device & Technical Information
•
Device type and operating system version
•
App crash logs and error reports (used for debugging only)
•
Push notification tokens (for alerts and reminders)
f. AI Chat Data
•
Messages and queries you send to the AI assistant within the app
•
These may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI) to generate responses
3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:

•
Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features
•
Manage your store inventory, sales, and business analytics
•
Send push notifications for important store events (low stock, new sales, etc.)
•
Enable team collaboration features
•
Process AI-assisted features such as product recognition and chat
•
Ensure account security and prevent unauthorized access
•
Comply with applicable laws and regulations
4. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

Third Party	Purpose
OpenAI / AI Providers	AI chat and image recognition features
Cloud Storage Provider	Secure storage of product images
Push Notification Service	Delivery of in-app notifications
Analytics Service	App performance monitoring (anonymized)
All third-party providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements. We only share the minimum data necessary.

5. Data Storage & Security
•
Your data is stored on secure servers protected by industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
•
Passwords are never stored in plain text
•
We use authentication tokens (Better Auth) to protect user sessions
•
Business data is stored in a secured database accessible only to authorized users of your store
•
We regularly review our security practices to maintain data integrity
6. Data Retention
•
Your account and business data is retained as long as your account is active
•
You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 9)
•
Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days of a deletion request
7. Permissions We Request
Permission	Reason

Storage/Media	Save and upload product images
Notifications	Send store alerts and reminders
Internet	Sync data and use cloud features
All permissions are optional where possible and are only used for the stated purpose.

Sari Store is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us personal data, we will promptly delete it.
